Our Mission

At Polyglot, we believe that learning a new language should be an exciting and engaging journey which builds confidence and lifelong skills rather than a chore with homework and tests. Our mission is to transform the way in which children and adults alike learn language by combining the standard vocab and grammar learning with a fun card game!

Our Purpose

Learning a new language is proven to improve brain and memory function. Our whole team has gone through the school system, learning languages in classrooms with often outdated techniques. We felt that by turning the process into a game rather than a chore, it would inspire the younger generation into expanding their horizons to living or even working overseas.
